Regression Model for predicting DELTA

Problem Definition
  • The Dataset consists of 6 Columns.
  • Dependent Column (the target) is (DELTA).
  • Independent Columns are (AGE, FC%, LC(ft/mile), TC(ft/mile), RUT(in)).
  • The Distribution of (AGE & RUT(in)) Columns is exponential Distribution, using box-cox tramsformation to optimize the Distribution to be Normal Distribution is better to help model learn and train correctly.
  • The Distribution of (DELTA (target)) is also exponential and containing zeros, taking the natural log(value+1) to also optimize the Distribution to be Normal, and after Model predicts the values to reverse what we did, by taking the (exponential(value) - 1).
  • The best Algorithm is (xgboost) an algorithm based on Adaboost (Algorithm) Understand Adaboost
  • The Algorithm achieve about r2-score = 67.5 % on validation Dataset.
